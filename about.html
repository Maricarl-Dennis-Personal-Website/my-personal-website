Songwriting

Creativity through songwriting nourishes my soul and my mind. I love the process of telling stories and expressing emotions through songwriting. I've written and produced songs for my personal projects and also for other people. I've released 2 EPs and several singles. I've also written curated songs from customer requests with a company called Songfinch. Music to me serves as as a cathartic release and a gift to encourage people tell their stories.



Rockclimbing

Rockclimbing at the gym is my kind of workout and play. Although I wouldn't call myself an expert and just rockclimb indoors for fun, I enjoy the mental and physical challenge of climbing. Visually evaluating a route, imagining how to climb each hold and actually doing it is such a fun process. I experience healthy frustration that fuels my determination when I fall and "fail." I also get to exercise my strength and muscles along the way, without even thinking too much about it!
